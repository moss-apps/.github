# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2024_

Moss Apps builds software with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains how our applications handle your data.

## Data Collection

**Moss Apps does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to external servers.** Our applications are designed to function entirely on your device.

- **Flick Player** — All playback, library scanning, and audio processing happens locally. No listening habits, play counts, or library contents leave your device.
- **Locker** — Your media stays encrypted on your device. We have no access to your vault contents, passwords, or encryption keys.
- **Moss Web** — The website does not use tracking cookies, analytics, or fingerprinting scripts.

## Third-Party Services

Our applications do not embed third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks. If a future application integrates a third-party service (e.g., for optional cloud sync), it will be clearly disclosed, opt-in, and accompanied by an updated policy.

## Crash Reporting

If our applications generate crash logs, those logs are stored locally and are never automatically uploaded. You may choose to share them when filing a bug report.

## Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy if our data practices change. Significant changes will be noted in the relevant app's release notes.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, open an issue in the relevant repository or visit [moss-apps](https://github.com/moss-apps).
